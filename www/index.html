This is a simple application where two people can deploy and sign a legal contract on the Ethereum network without the need for a 3rd party. Once the contract has been signed by both parties, it cannot be altered and will forever exist on the Ethereum blockchain. A contract can only be updated by the two parties(addresses) included in the contract. A contract can be modified until only until both sides are in signed status. Once a contract has been signed by both parties, it cannot be modified. The contract document file is stored in IPFS(Infura).
